Find environments that are archived and no longer active.

## Goal

Locate archived environments and review their resources when needed.

## What you need

- The correct project selected
- Permission to view archived environments

## Using Devopness MCP

Try these prompts in Devopness MCP:

- "List archived environments in `acme-platform`"
- "Show archived environments in my current project"

## What you see

- Archived environments listed separately from active ones
- Enough detail to open one before restoring or removing it

## Verify

- The archived list shows the environment you expected
- You can open it and review its state

## Common issues

- The archived list is empty: confirm the environment is archived first
- You cannot view details: confirm your role and permission set

- [Unarchive an environment](/docs/environments/unarchive-environment)
